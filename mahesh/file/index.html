About Me

# I Makes Your Bike Happy

Hello! My name is Mahesh Manoj. I am a passionate bike technician, a wandering bikepacker, an enthusiastic photographer and a solo traveller. In 2018, I dropped my college to travel and experience more. In spite of all hardships, I worked relentlessly hard to make my dream come true. And in 2019 at an age of 19, I finally did a bikepacking to Nepal from Kochi. The after effects of this ride were that I was finally able to decide what career should I choose and it was to become a bike technician. I am a successful professional performance bike technician now.
